About

Three-sided is produced by me, Samy. The intent of the show is to provide forcibly clear discourse to the viewers. Often we talk in circles and constantly move goalposts, in the end making no progress with the exception of confusing everyone involved. Not here. Our number one priority is clarity. If by the end of the video you do not understand the fundamental difference in perspectives that everything stems from, we haven't done our job.
